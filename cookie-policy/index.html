1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser when you visit a website. They are commonly used for session management, preferences, analytics, and advertising.

2. Cookies We Use

This website (smartdailyapps.github.io) does not set any first-party cookies. We do not use:

  • Tracking or analytics cookies (no Google Analytics, no Plausible, no Hotjar)
  • Advertising or retargeting cookies
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Any cookie that identifies you personally

4. The MyGoldFolio App

The Android application stores your data exclusively on your device using Android's local storage (AsyncStorage). It does not use cookies and does not communicate with any SmartDailyApps server. The only network requests the App makes are to third-party gold price APIs to fetch live prices.

5. Your Browser Controls

You can manage, block, or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Since this website does not set tracking cookies, blocking cookies will not affect your ability to use this site.
