Smart scenario generator

Auto-generates 6 price scenarios from 30-day trend analysis (Bull/Bear/Sideways). ATH and break-even injection.

3-source price fallback

v1.5.8

Automatic fallthrough across 3 built-in sources. No API keys needed.

8 currencies & 3 weight units

EUR, USD, GBP, INR, AED, SAR, TRY, EGP. Gram, troy oz, and tola. Switch at any time — all screens update instantly. Live exchange rate cache (1h TTL).

Named buy/sell margin sets for different dealers. Assign a margin profile per holding.

Smart history retention

v1.9.2

Intelligent pruning keeps recent data granular, older data summarised. No manual cleanup needed.

Extended BG fetch schedules

v1.9.2

Daily, weekly, or monthly background price checks in addition to hourly.
