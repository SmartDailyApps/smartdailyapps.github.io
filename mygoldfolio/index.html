Features

Everything you need to
track your gold

Six powerful modules. One lightweight app.

Live Gold Price

EUR per gram from 3 built-in free sources with automatic fallthrough. Buy rate, sell rate, and spread — all updated on demand. 24h high/low window, price history chart with crosshair, 7d/30d/90d change strips.

Portfolio P&L

Track every holding with live profit/loss, return %, break-even price, and configurable tax-free countdown. List, compact, and timeline views. FULLY SOLD badge on cleared holdings, remaining gram counts on partial sells.

Smart Scenarios

Auto-generated what-if price scenarios based on 30-day trend analysis. Six trend classes from Strong Bull to Strong Bear, plus ATH injection and break-even anchoring. Interactive slider simulates any price in real time.

Smart Alerts

Price watchers, break-even crossing, tax-free anniversary, portfolio milestones, and P&L delta watchers. Alerts fire in the foreground and via background fetch — with a full in-app alert history log.

Per-Holding Sell Tracking

Sell from any individual holding with exact grams. Partial sells supported. Realised vs unrealised P&L breakdown, per-sale profit figures, and tax report CSV export — all without touching your original purchase records.

Privacy & Security

Biometric or PIN lock with exponential backoff after failed attempts. One-tap privacy mode hides all monetary values app-wide. Full JSON backup and restore. Nothing ever leaves your phone.
